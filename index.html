The AI-Powered Solution

We're developing computer vision and AI technologies to transform plant phenotyping:

Automated Trait Analysis

Machine learning algorithms that can automatically measure plant height, leaf area, disease resistance, and other crucial traits from images.

Real-time Monitoring

Continuous tracking of plant growth and development, enabling early detection of issues and faster breeding cycles.

Data-Driven Decisions

Advanced analytics to help breeders make more informed selections and accelerate the breeding process.
